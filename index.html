08 / Policy Brief · Live on GitHub

Forecasting Swedish Crop Yields to 2050 — XGBoost & SHAP

Best for roles: Climate Data Analyst · Climate Policy Analyst

Business problem
Agricultural ministries need yield forecasts under climate scenarios to plan adaptation policy.
My role
Reproduced a Lund baseline and extended it with explainability + scenario layer.
Method
21 län × 18 crops × 55 years; four-model comparison, holdout-tuned hyperparameters, SHAP explainability, RCP 8.5 choropleth.

Business value
Shows climate-data analysis and decision-support thinking applied to public policy.
