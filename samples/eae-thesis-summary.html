Project context

Rural farmers in target regions struggle to access the sustainable agriculture knowledge they need to adapt to climate variability and shifting market expectations. VerdeWise is a multilingual digital learning platform — offline-capable, audio-visual, AI-supported, and farmer-centred — designed to close that gap. The thesis builds the complete project plan to deliver it.
